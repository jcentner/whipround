# Code of Conduct

Whipround is a small, public, tribute-driven project. The whole thing only works
if it's a kind place. This applies to issues, suggestions, pull requests, and
any other space tied to this repo.

## The short version

- **Be kind and assume good faith.** We're here to thank people, not dunk on them.
- **Tributes are about appreciation.** No campaigns or comments that are
  mean-spirited, even affectionately.
- **Respect beneficiaries.** Don't @-mention or pressure a potential beneficiary —
  blessing outreach is handled privately, before anything goes public.
- **No harassment** of any kind: no slurs, threats, demeaning comments, sexualized
  language, doxxing, or sustained disruption.
- **Keep it on-topic.** No political campaigning or commercial promotion dressed up
  as a tribute.

## Not tolerated

Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, sharing others' private information,
and spam. Suggestions that target a living person without their blessing, or that
are commercial or political, will be closed.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, label, hide, or delete any contribution and may block
accounts that break these rules. Reports go to **conduct@write-it-right.ai** —
we'll respond as quickly as we reasonably can, and we'll keep reporters
confidential.
